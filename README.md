## 🏠 Project: Real Estate Marketing Campaign

### 📌 Overview
A multi‑channel real estate marketing campaign designed to increase property visibility, generate qualified leads, and support sales teams through data‑driven insights and structured reporting.

This project combined **administrative coordination, digital marketing analytics, and performance reporting** to optimize campaign execution and decision‑making.

---

### 🎯 Objectives
- Increase brand and property awareness across digital platforms  
- Generate and track qualified buyer and investor leads  
- Support sales teams with accurate, timely performance reports  
- Improve campaign efficiency through data analysis and optimization  

---

### 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- Google Analytics  
- Meta Business Suite (Facebook & Instagram Ads)  
- Power BI & Microsoft Excel  
- Google Sheets  
- CRM & HubSpot  
- Canva (creative assets)  

---

### 🔑 Key Responsibilities
- Coordinated campaign timelines, tasks, and deliverables across teams  
- Managed digital marketing performance tracking and reporting  
- Created dashboards and reports for lead generation and conversion metrics  
- Monitored ad spend, engagement, and ROI  
- Supported stakeholder communication and documentation  
- Maintained organized campaign records and compliance documentation  

---

### 📊 Deliverables
- Campaign performance dashboards (Power BI / Excel)  
- Weekly and monthly marketing reports  
- Lead tracking spreadsheets and CRM updates  
- Process documentation and campaign summaries  

---

### ✅ Outcomes
- Improved campaign visibility and lead engagement  
- Enabled data‑driven adjustments to improve ad performance  
- Provided sales teams with clear insights to support closing activities  
- Enhanced operational efficiency through structured reporting  

---

Marketing Analytics • Data Visualization • Project Coordination  
Stakeholder Communication • Reporting & Dashboards
